French Onion Soup

Descriprtion

Caramelized onions, served with a base of beef soup and a mix of cheeses. Perfectly complemented by a slice of garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 6 large red or yellow onions (about 3 pounds), peeled and thinly sliced root to stem (see How to Slice an Onion), about 10 cups of sliced onions total
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • kosher salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups of beefstock, chicken stock, or a combination of the two (Traditionally the soup is made with beef stock).
  • 1/2 cups dry vermouth or dry white wine
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons brandy
  • 8 slices french bread or baguette
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Gruyere
  • Sprinkling of Parmesan

Steps

  1. Caramelize Onions
  2. Deglaze the por with vermouth or wine
  3. Add the stock, bay leaves, and thyme
  4. Toast the French bread slices
  5. Serve
